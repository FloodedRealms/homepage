Why does this site look like complete garbage?

Because I am a big believer in the the indie web. There was a time before the walled gardens of social media, substack, and other conglomerated online services. This is a callback to that time. Anyone can have their own, personalized, landing site on the web. More interesting and more expressive than any facebook / twitter / mewe profile can ever be. Even more than the myspace of old. However, I am not a graphic design artist, I am a back-end developer. So that means that my site looks like it was designed by someone who should never be allowed to design anything. Because it was. However, this should give you hope! Anyone can have a webpage, and people should. It isn't a beauty contest.
